About Kazuhiko "Smokey" Nagata

Who is he

Smokey Nagata is a Japanese business visionary, specialist and driver, who is known as the ruler of the vehicle field. He came into the eyes of individuals when he founded the organization Top Secret, which has created effective float vehicles that have won awards around the world.

Age

Smokey Nagata was born on February 25, 1964 in Japan. He will be 58 years old from about 2022.

Occupation

He is arguably the most outrageous tuner to ever come out of Japan. He landed a job as a mechanic for Toyota and some of that is a result of the crazy vehicles he's built, and it's thanks to the ridiculously dangerous, extremely illegal speed limits he's done over the long haul.

Arrest

Smokey Nagata was imprisoned in 1998, according to one source. That year he was trying to get on a British highway at 200 mph. Nagata reached a speed of 290 km/h, was detained and spent a night in prison.
